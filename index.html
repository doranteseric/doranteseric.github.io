"Finding peace one line of code at a time" - Eric Dorantes

Hello, I'm Eric Dorantes, a passionate and self-taught software engineer with a background in mobile app development and a newfound focus on web development. My journey began over a decade ago when I started exploring the world of programming on my own. After taking a brief hiatus, I dove back into the tech world, this time immersing myself in the intricacies of web technologies. Over the past year, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React, while also pursuing an associate degree in web development at Long Beach City College. Currently, I am the sole web developer at my company, working in a dynamic, startup-like environment. I've officially been accepted into CSUDH Computer Science Program, from Viking to Toro!
